ABOUT MINZ




Damilola Aminu better known by stage name Minz, is a Nigerian recording artiste, songwriter and producer. He released his hit single in July 2016, called "Aunty Patricia". The song was produced by Mowizzy and Minz, himself. On September 11, Minz released the official video for the much anticipated song "Aunty Patricia" directed by TG Omori from Omori Views. For latest updates, follow Minz on Social Media @MinzNSE.

Music

My Music

I began recording 2 years ago. But i've been doing music for 5 years. In fact, at an early age I started writing, and when I was in secondary school I used to rap. My most recent singles are "Odoyewu" and Story". "Odoyewu" was produced by my friend, Mowizzy, and myself. "Story" was selected by the popular music magazine The Native Mag as song of the week. "Talk" is also one of my singles and was selected as one of the top songs on naijapals.com. I'm very grateful to everyone supporting the song. "Aunty Patricia" is another recent single. I was making beats with Mowizzy (my close friend and producer), and we decided to come up with something creative. We began vibing to our beats and then recorded the single Aunty Patricia. The song now has over 100k listens on SoundCloud. Don't forget to checkout its music video on YouTube. I am currently not signed to a label, but working with a promotion/management company called Purple Tunes based in the US. Expect more from me in the nearest future as I plan to release more sounds and visuals very soon!
