Aunty Patricia Music Video

Aunty Patricia Music Video was directed by TG Omori from Omori Views. The Cover Art was made by April Maey @aprilmaey . Video shoot was held in Ikoyi, Lagos State, Nigeria. Several music blogs picked up the release for publicity, including Tayotv, notjustok, Jaguda, 360nobs, and Pulse.ng.

Aunty Patricia

I was making beats with Mowizzy (my close friend and producer), and we decided to come up with something creative. We began vibing to our beats and then recorded the single Aunty Patricia. The song now has over 100k listens on SoundCloud. Also, around 7k listens on Spotify.
